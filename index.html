Protein Modelling for Bio- and Food- Industry Applications


Protearix is focussed on molecular-simulation modelling of bio-, pharma- and food-industry problems (e.g., protein coagulation), and leveraging its great deal of experience and expertise in these areas to complement real-world trouble-shooting in industry operations. Coupled to these industry-consultancy services is leading, inter alia, first-principles, simulation-led design of synthetic proteins and nanoparticles for a variety of protein-engineering applications.

Particular expertise are:

  • (Non-equilibrium) Molecular dynamics
  • Protein (anti)agglomeration
  • Solubility Prediction
  • Drug discovery
